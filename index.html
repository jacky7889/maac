Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria?

Any student who has completed 10+2 or equivalent can apply. For advanced courses, basic computer knowledge is recommended.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We have a dedicated placement cell with 50+ industry partners. 90% of our students get placed within 3 months of course completion.

What is the duration of courses?

Courses range from 6 to 12 months depending on the specialization. Weekend batches are also available.

Do you have online classes?

Yes, we offer both online and offline modes. Our hybrid learning model ensures flexibility for all students.
